Books and long-form
======

* **[Macroeconomics with R](https://macrowithr.com)** — a 14-chapter Quarto book covering UK, European, and international macroeconomic data analysis in R. Uses the `ons`, `boe`, `hmrc`, `obr`, `readecb`, `readoecd`, and `inflateR` packages. Topics include time series, the Phillips curve, monetary policy, nowcasting, VARs, fiscal policy, trade, and climate macroeconomics.
