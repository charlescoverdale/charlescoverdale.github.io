## 2026

**Coverdale, C.** (2026). *mpshock: An R Package for Cross-Country Monetary Policy Shock Analysis.* Working paper, April 2026. \[[PDF](/files/coverdale_mpshock_2026.pdf)\] \[[code](https://github.com/charlescoverdale/mpshock)\]

Introduces `mpshock`, an R package that bundles thirteen monetary policy shock and stance series across the United States, United Kingdom, and Australia. Series span five identification families (narrative, high-frequency, sign-restriction, informationally-robust, shadow-rate term-structure) and include the bundled shocks of Nakamura and Steinsson (2018), Bauer and Swanson (2023), Gürkaynak, Sack, and Swanson (2005), Jarociński and Karadi (2020), Miranda-Agrippino and Ricco (2021), Wu and Xia (2016), Braun, Miranda-Agrippino, and Saha (2025), Cesa-Bianchi, Thwaites, and Vicondoa (2020), Cloyne and Hürtgen (2016), Hambur and Haque (2023), and Beckers (2020). Available on CRAN.
